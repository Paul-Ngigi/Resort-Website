Accomodations

Our rooms and suites are uniquely and elegantly decorated to reflect traditional architecture.
Each of them is equipped with air-conditioning, an en-suite bathroom and a Swahili-bed.
The rooms are placed in a perfect position where guests can enjoy the ocean view regardless
being at any room.
